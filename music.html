Live Music

Come join us at Bullquarian Brewhouse for live music multiple times a week: From local groups such as The Blarneys to any number of travelling artsists such as Ethan Keller, or The Ex-Bombers.

The Bullquarian Brewhouse and friends sponsor an Open Mic Night the third Thursday of every month. Inquire at the bar for further details!
